The trip in a nutshell

A journey beyond the usual, combining everything: From the capital of the Spanish Regency of the Americas and today's capital of Peru, Lima, to the unique experience of the Amazon jungle in Puerto Maldonado, and from there to the "archaeological capital of the Americas", Cusco, and the most important cities built by the Incas along the Sacred Valley of Vilcanota: Pisac, Oyadaitambo and the majestic Machu Picchu! From Puno and the floating islands of the Uros tribe in the deep blue Lake Titicaca, the "highest" and largest floating lake in the world, we come to Bolivia and are amazed in Tiahuanaco and the capital La Paz. Returning to Peru, we tour Puno and Arequipa, and via Lima we come to the wondrous wetlands of the Bayestas Islands, and the mysterious lines of Naska!

Highlights
  • A rare overnight stay in the Sacred Valley to have all day and time to enjoy more of the area where the Incas built their most important cities
  • Flight from Lima to Arequipa and stay in Arequipa to avoid thousands of kilometers and inconvenience
  • Visit and stay in Arequipa, the second largest city in Peru
  • Visit to the richest rainforest on earth, that of the Amazon with activities in the jungle
  • Cruise to the Bayestas Islands
  • Half board daily, in the Amazon full board
