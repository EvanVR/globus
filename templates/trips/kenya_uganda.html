Highlights

We start our journey from the capital Kampala, built on seven hills on the shores of Lake Victoria. Continuing into Uganda's interior we observe white rhinos at Ziwa Sanctuary and take in the spectacle at Merchison Falls that form the waters of the Nile. We are then treated to a chimpanzee safari in Kibale National Park, Kanyanchu Park with its black and white colombus monkeys and arrive at the famous Queen Elizabeth National Park where we have two overnight stays. We will boat on the Kazinga Channel, transfer to the park's Ishasa sector to see the rare climbing lions, enter the Inaccessible Buindi Forest where we will also meet the Pygmy Batwa, an ancient tribe of hunter-gatherers. In Buindi we will also have the thrilling experience offered only by a gorilla safari, an experience of a lifetime that will move us and will certainly make us see many things in a different light. From Lake Mutanta we approach the Bhaghinga National Park with its rare golden monkeys. Finally we fly to Nairobi to enter Kenya for a journey "Beyond Africa": a tour of Kenya's stunning parks and beautiful lakes - Masai Mara, Nakuru and Naivashawith.
