The trip in a nutshell

The puzzle of Argentina is simply captivating: Stretching from the southern border of Bolivia to the southernmost tip of Latin America, flirting with both the Atlantic and almost to the edge of the Antarctic Circle, it captivates visitors with breathtaking natural landscapes, a sweet and welcoming people and a culture that... swirls to the rhythm of the bandoneon and the lasso of the gauchos.

Gear up and... let's go! Nature lovers have no choice but to stock up on all kinds of equipment. A raincoat is essential once they are under the Garganta do Diabo (Devil's Throat), to surrender to the eruption of the "Big Water", the Iguacu Falls, on the country's border with Brazil and Uruguay.

What to expect

Our journey starts from the "port of good winds", Buenos Aires, where tango was born, where Argentina itself begins. We enjoy the city with extensive guided tours, reaching the Tigre Delta and then continuing on to the famous Iguassu Falls, the natural border between Argentina and Brazil. We stay in the area and tour the falls from both sides before moving on to the most sensual, controversial and colourful city, Rio de Janeiro, which has become identified in the world's consciousness with fun, frolic, football and dancing!
