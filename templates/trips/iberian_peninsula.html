The trip in a nutshell

The philosophy of our European trips is to offer a uniquely organized program, original, complete and designed as a full day travel experience that you will never forget. Especially on this trip you will experience Lisbon from the inside, with our experienced and qualified guide who will be with you all the time. We have organized a specially designed walking tour of the hidden beauties of the city, known only to the locals and the places where the great Portuguese poet Fernando Pessoa lived. On this tour we will also use, together with our guide, the city's very scenic public transport, such as the cog railway and tram, for a completely genuine and authentic experience. As part of this Lisbon experience, we visit neighboring Evora, a museum city, which is an entire UNESCO monument. In addition, we have an extensive Barcelona tour program, complete with a full day excursion to the magnificent Gerona and the picturesque town of Figueres with a visit to the Salvador Dali Museum.

What to expect

On this trip we delve into Lisbon but also follow in the footsteps of the world-famous poet Fernando Pessoa, who praised it in his own unique way. In addition we visit its magical surroundings on the Atlantic. We go to the very expensive and luxurious 19th century royal resorts of Estoril, Cascais, Sidra and the westernmost tip of Europe, Cabo da Roca. We spend a full day in the very historic Evora, a city designated as a UNESCO "museum". We also see the traditional coastal villages of Obidos, Nazaré, Alcobasa and the gardens at Quinta dos Lourintos.

Our next stop is Barcelona, famous for its architecture and especially for the buildings of Antoni Gaudí, which are all UNESCO monuments. Next is the medieval city of Gerona, one of the most beautiful in Catalonia, and Figueres - home of Salvador Dali. There is the museum of the surrealist painter with the largest collection of his works in the world.
