The trip in a nutshell

Mexico is already known for its magnificent archaeological sites, its wonderful nature, its unique architecture. But on this trip, we want to go beyond the familiar, the known Mexico. To explore the authentic, traditional Mexico, as it unfolds through its legends and its unique, colonial architecture. We expect to be close to dramatic and unpredictable nature, to experience the countryside with its imaginative architecture, to wander through cobbled squares and alleys where life seems to have fallen dormant, to wander through Morelia, to enjoy the sunset in colonial-style towns, in a time travel experience unlike any other. The addition of Taxco and Cuernavaca, and the stay in Puebla are added value to the trip, which you will only find at Globus. What to expect
  • A wonderful trip to Mexico, encapsulating some of the most important elements of its history, culture and beautiful nature.
  • An itinerary that takes us from Mexico City to the most important pilgrimage of Latin Americans - Our Lady of Guadalupe - and perhaps the holiest site of the pre-Columbian civilizations, Teotihuacan.
  • We then come to Queretaro, one of the 15 "most authentic cities in the world", San Miguel de Ayente and Guanajuato, to arrive via Pachquaro in colourful, traditional Morelia. After our stay and tour of Morelia, we return to Mexico City to visit the house-museum of Frida Kahlo. Next are three colonial gems, Cuernavaca, Taxco and Puebla, the last one we stay in to enhance the experience.
