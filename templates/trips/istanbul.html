The trip in a nutshell

A colourful, bustling, intoxicating city, at the meeting point of two continents - Europe and Asia - and two seas - the Black Sea and the Mediterranean.

A city with a wonderful natural setting and a spectacular topography.

The memories of its millennial history, the richness of its monuments, its picturesque old quarters and the modern face of the city give it a unique charm. With its gaze fixed on the West and its soul given to the East, it fuses the most resonant contrasts in its innermost being. Skyscrapers are mixed with minarets, sirens with the voice of the muezzin, kismet with programming. The European orientation of the city is in constant tug-of-war with the Asian roots of its population. A city built on seven hills and surrounded on three sides by water, its magic intertwined with legends, history and poetry.

A city that holds an exceptional place in world history. A city of many names: New Rome, Constantine's City, Queen of Cities, Queen's Palace.
