The trip in a nutshell

Since the first Globus tours, this trip has had hundreds of events under its belt, the result of which is this well-planned tour of Sri Lanka. Always on the lines of Globus Travel, a comprehensive program with quality features, so that we get to know Sri Lanka on a rich trek from Colombo, Polonnaruwa and Anuradapura to Gale and Yala Park in the south! With a thoughtful selection of overnight stays in areas with beautiful hotels rather than others without tourist infrastructure. We also dedicate a stay in Yala Park to explore Southeastern Sri Lanka with greater ease. With proper duration - not "truncated" to cut costs in a country where road trips are particularly time consuming. Elephants, archaeological treasures, mystical atmosphere, lush green trails, exotic beaches in a trip overflowing with exoticism. The excellent, quality standards of our program and our many years of experience in the region guarantee an unforgettable excursion.

The trip also includes
  • A whole day dedicated to the very interesting capital of Sri Lanka, Colombo
  • Entrances to the attractions in Sri Lanka that have a high cost but that does not lead us to reduce the visits
  • Overnight stay in Polonnaruwa to enjoy the great archaeological site
  • Visit to the iconic site of Anuradapura in northern Sri Lanka
  • Two overnight stays on Sri Lanka's excellent beaches to allow us to enjoy the tropical atmosphere, relax at the end of the trip in a paradise environment drowned in coconut trees
  • A boat safari in the lush river vegetation of the area, a safari that Globus has discovered and offers you - an on-board journey of observing not only the tropical nature of the area but also local life as it develops on the river banks
  • Accommodation in the quaint colonial town of Nuwara Eliya with its tea plantations
  • 4x4 jeep safari to Yala Park, the largest and richest park in the country to get in touch with the natural environment and the animal kingdom of the country
  • Stay in the country's largest park, Yala Park
  • Show with local dances and walking on the charcoal in Kandy
  • Visit to the beaches of Beruvella and stay in the Vaskaduwa area, with surprise activities, courtesy of Globus
  • Visit to picturesque Gale
