The trip in a nutshell

In the most luxurious way we arrive in Australia, we land in Sydney and after seeing Melbourne we depart for Auckland and then - with a domestic flight - to Queenstown, in order to see the most important parts of the largest island in the world. The exuberance of the program is immediately apparent to the experienced traveler: daily half board, the Blue Mountains excursion and much more...

Highlights
  • Two-day trip to Ayers Rock.
  • Train excursion to Kuranda Rainforest and ride the famous Skyrail over the rainforest.
  • Catamaran cruise to the wonder of nature, the Great Coral Reef with seafood buffet lunch, snorkeling, fish feeding and other activities.
  • Half board throughout the program, especially important for Australia.
  • A unique excursion to the Blue Mountains from Sydney and a visit to a park with koalas, kangaroos etc.
