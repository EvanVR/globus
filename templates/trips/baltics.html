The trip in a nutshell

A journey that has become synonymous with Globus, thanks to our guaranteed weekly departures every summer to the three Baltic countries: Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania. With the right duration, without cuts, so that we can have a thorough, rich trip, with half board daily and with entrances to many attractions. This trip is first and foremost a trip back in time. With medieval buildings with roofs dating back to the age of the knights and facades that run the gamut of pastel colors, narrow streets, cobbled roads, impressive castles, tranquil lakes and beautiful routes, this itinerary has all the right ingredients to form a sensational experience in a Europe you can't imagine exists.

What to expect

A rich program in the three Baltic States: Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania. Estonia's capital, beautiful Tallinn and Estonia's oldest city Tartu, Latvia's capital Riga which is one of the best preserved cities in Europe, the Lithuanian capital Vilnius, with the neighboring city of Trakai (Lithuania's first capital) and Kaunas are our stops on this wonderful journey.

Entrance to nine impressive sights of the Baltic countries, which are omitted in other programs, such as the Trakai castle and its museum, the Chiourlionis museum in Kaunas, the Rudale palace, the antique car museum in Riga, the medieval castle of Turida, the castle and museum of Vittautauta, the summer palace of Peter the Great in Tallinn, and the restored church of St John with its unique terracotta statuettes in Tartu.
