The trip in a nutshell

One of the most complete programs of the market in one of the most famous destinations in the world! Pyramids, mummies, pharaohs, historical monuments of different eras, magnificent mosques, Christian churches, monastic centers, bazaars, villages and towns along the Nile and much more include our short or longer trips, always designed with every detail and with the best facilities. Images different, images colourful and... bustling!

What to expect

Our tour of Egypt starts of course in Cairo, the capital and largest city of the country. We will visit Cairo's famous archaeological museum, the old city, the Al Khalili market and of course the archaeological site of Giza with its pyramids and Sphinx, one of the most recognizable images on the planet. We then come to Alexandria to see the famous Library, which aspires to have the splendor of its Ptolemaic counterpart, as well as the catacombs and the city's seafront. We then fly to Luxor, with the magnificent temples at Karnak and on the west bank of the Nile with the Valley of the Kings, and sail to the Aswan Dam and the artificial Lake Nasser.

We opt for a 5-day cruise on the Nile, on a luxury cruise ship, in order to have the time required to allow us to get to know in detail the archaeological world of Egypt. With half board throughout the program (and full board on the cruise ship) and full days, with in-depth guided tours, and generally a full program with no optional tours or dinners, so that you can be cost assured from the start. One thing is for sure, Egypt, mother of brilliant cultures, offers the traveler powerful experiences.
