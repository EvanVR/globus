The trip in a nutshell

The trip in West Papua in not a simple trip, it's a lifetime experience and a demandind traveler that wants to get the most of this experience know that only Globus can provide that.

The island Papua or New Guinea is located it north of Australia and is one of the biggest islands on the plante. Its history is odd and perturbed, since it was conquered by the Dutchs, Germans, Australians and British. Today is devided into two parts: East Papua, which is an independent nation and West Papua, which belong to Indonesia. A unique feature of this place is that even today is preserves its original and wild character. And when we say "wild" we mean both its nature and its inhabitants.

  • Boat ride in Brazza river with tropical flora.
  • Trecking in the rainforest in Sentic.
  • Overnight stay, boat rides and snorkeling in Raja Ampat sea. One of the most beautiful corners of the planet that we discover and totally recommend
  • Visit to Maluku islands
  • Visit tribe villages
  • Overnight stay in Kabui bay where we'll see the famous coral reefs
The trip also includes
  • Special permission to enter Papua
  • Donations to the villages
